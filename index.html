About Sam's Cafe

A Half Moon Bay Institution

Tucked right along the coast in Half Moon Bay, Sam's Cafe has been a morning ritual for locals and a welcome discovery for visitors. It's the kind of place where you sit down, breathe in the salty air, and feel at home before your coffee even arrives.

"Simple, cozy diner with delicious breakfast options. Service was friendly and we enjoyed everything we ordered."

The menu is focused and honest — classic breakfast plates, fresh-squeezed juices, and a clam chowder that regulars swear is the real deal. Everything's made with care and served without pretense.
